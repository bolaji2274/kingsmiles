About Us

Kings Miles International Limited (RC 1377326) is an indigenous Nigerian company incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act, 2004. We are a share-holding company engaged in general contracts, sales, marketing, manufacturing, trading, distribution, commission agency, manufacturing representation, consulting and general supply.

Our Focus

Our primary objective is agricultural development. We specialize in agricultural farming, agro-allied services and farm implements. Our Managing Director's commitment to excellence and integrity propelled rapid growth since inception.

Agricultural Solutions

To address Nigeria's food shortages and insecurity, we import, sell and service high-quality agricultural produce, modern tractors and implements. Additionally, we supply inorganic fertilizers and represent various manufacturers. Our goal is to support subsistent and commercial farmers, enhancing agricultural productivity.
